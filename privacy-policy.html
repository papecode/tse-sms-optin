Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

1. Introduction

The Socratic Experience ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the TSE Academy Open House SMS registration service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and register for SMS reminders.

2. Information We Collect

When you register for SMS reminders about the TSE Academy Open House, we collect the following information:

  • Phone number (required)
  • Your explicit consent to receive SMS messages

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the phone number you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • Sending SMS reminders about the TSE Academy Open House event
  • Sending event updates and related communications
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Your Phone Number is Not Shared

Your phone number is not sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties. We do not use third-party contact lists or data brokers. Your phone number is used exclusively by The Socratic Experience to send you SMS reminders about the TSE Academy Open House.

5. SMS Opt-In and Opt-Out

You have explicitly opted in to receive SMS messages by checking the consent checkbox on our registration form. You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying "STOP" to any SMS message you receive. You will not be charged for replying "STOP" or "HELP" to SMS messages.

6. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. The frequency of messages will depend on event scheduling. Standard carrier rates and fees apply for all SMS communications.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your phone number against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your phone number for the duration of the event reminder campaign. After the TSE Academy Open House event concludes, we may retain your information for compliance and record-keeping purposes, or we may delete it upon your request.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will promptly delete such information.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of SMS communications by replying "STOP"

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: contact@socraticexperience.com

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date above.
